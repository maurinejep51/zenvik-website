WHY CHOOSE ZENVIK — BACKGROUND DEPTH ENHANCEMENT

Enhance only the background of the Why Choose Zenvik section.

Do not modify:

* Content
* Cards
* Icons
* Layout
* Animations
* Typography

Only improve the background atmosphere.

==================================================

OBJECTIVE

Create a premium enterprise technology environment.

The background should reinforce:

* Innovation
* Infrastructure
* Technology
* Reliability

without introducing visible devices or distracting graphics.

==================================================

TECHNOLOGY LIGHT COLUMNS

Create 3 to 5 vertical technology light columns.

Requirements:

* Positioned behind the content
* Different heights
* Different widths
* Soft edges
* Low opacity

Colors:

Primary:
#043a7e

Secondary:
#0f172a

Highlight:
#dfa408

==================================================

LIGHT BEHAVIOR

Each light column should:

* Fade upward naturally
* Fade into the section background
* Appear as a technology energy source
* Create depth

Do not create:

* Solid beams
* Neon tubes
* Harsh gradients
* Bright streaks

==================================================

COLUMN PLACEMENT

Suggested placement:

* One behind left content area
* Two behind card grid
* One near bottom-right
* One near upper-right

Keep placement asymmetrical.

Do not create evenly spaced columns.

==================================================

GOLD ACCENT LIGHTS

Add occasional gold highlights within the columns.

Requirements:

* Very subtle
* Small sections only
* Not entire columns
* Opacity below 10%

Color:

#dfa408

Secondary accent:

#7a6200

==================================================

BACKGROUND MOTION

Very subtle motion only.

Requirements:

* Slow light breathing effect
* 8–12 second cycle
* Minimal brightness variation

Do not create:

* Moving beams
* Flashing effects
* Pulsing backgrounds
* Visible animation loops

The movement should be almost imperceptible.

==================================================

DEPTH LAYERS

Create three background layers:

Layer 1:
Dark gradient base

Layer 2:
Technology light columns

Layer 3:
Soft atmospheric glow

This creates depth without clutter.

==================================================

VISUAL GOAL

The section should feel like it exists inside a premium technology environment.

The background should suggest:

* Enterprise infrastructure
* Cloud platforms
* Innovation
* Digital transformation

without showing:

* Laptops
* Computer screens
* Servers
* Data centers
* Technology devices

The effect should be subtle, premium, modern, and support the trust-focused nature of the Why Choose Zenvik section.
